## 7. Additional Considerations

### Defensive Registrations
Consider whether to also register:
- **ADMIRAL** (standalone, in relevant classes) — broader protection but higher likelihood of office action due to existing "Admiral" marks
- **THE ADMIRAL FRAMEWORK** — with definite article, as used in some materials

### Domain Names
- Check availability of `admiralframework.com` and related domains
- Consider defensive registration of common misspellings

### International Filing
- If international commercialization is planned, consider Madrid Protocol filing within 6 months of US filing to claim US priority date
- Key markets to consider: EU (EUIPO), UK (UKIPO), Singapore (IPOS — given IMDA governance framework activity)
